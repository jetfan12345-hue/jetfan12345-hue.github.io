Origin Story

Don founded DonCorp after a legendary conversation that went: "What if I had a website?" and then immediately "What if it was funny?"

With zero board meetings and one (1) coffee, DonCorp was born — a practice project to learn GitHub Pages without pretending to be a Fortune 500 company. We failed at not pretending. That's the joke.
