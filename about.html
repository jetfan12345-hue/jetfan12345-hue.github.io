Our Mission

To make drying clothes feel like saving the world — even when it's just string.

The Problem

Traditional dryers use electricity. Electricity comes from... places. Those places sometimes burn things. We asked ourselves: what if we didn't burn things?

The answer was obvious: use the sun. The sun has been free for 4.6 billion years and has never sent us a bill.

Our Solution

After months of R&D (Research & Duct-tape), SunLine Technologies™ invented the X1: a revolutionary solar drying system that requires zero watts because it is, functionally, a clothesline with better branding.

We package it in cardboard because cardboard is recyclable, and also because that's what we had.
