Our Mission

To make harnessing the sun's power as simple and effective as possible.

The Problem

Conventional dryers draw significant electricity with every load. That energy often comes from sources that impact the environment and your monthly bill.

Meanwhile, the sun delivers abundant, free thermal energy every clear day — energy we're already receiving whether we use it or not.

Our Solution

SunLine Technologies™ designed the X1 as a straightforward solar drying system: position it outdoors, hang your laundry, and let sunlight and natural airflow do the work. No motor. No heating element. No utility draw.

It's not complicated technology — it's effective design that puts the sun back in charge of drying your clothes.
